Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

A yummy American favorite. Great for a quick lunch or snack!

Ingredients

  • bread of your choice (recommendation: thick cut whole wheat bread)
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jam or Jelly (Strawberry Jam is a favorite of mine!

Steps

  1. Toast two slices of bread (If thickly cut, try pan frying with a bit of butter!)
  2. Spread jam on one side of the toasted bread.
  3. Spread peanut butter on the other side.
  4. Enjoy!
