Japchae

A sweet and savory Korean favorite. Easily made vegan by omitting meat or seafood

Ingredients

  • Korean Glass Noodles
  • Roasted Sesame Seeds
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sweetener of your Choice
  • Red Pepper Chili Flakes

Steps

  1. Boil noodles for around 10 minutes or until al dente.
  2. Combine spices, liquids and Sesame Seeds in a cup, then stir.
  3. Strain noodles and immediately combine sauce
  4. Enjoy!
